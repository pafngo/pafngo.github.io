About Us

Mission & Vision

Mission

Founded by Nitesh Ray, Pehli Aasha Foundation is dedicated to uplifting underprivileged communities through education, health services, and social welfare programs.


Vision

To create a society where every individual has the opportunity to lead a dignified life.

Current Projects & Drives

Clean Up India Drive 2025

The Pehli Aasha Foundation, in collaboration with @silly.sensei and powered by @naturalhege, is leading a clean-up drive on May 4, 2025, in South Moti Bagh, New Delhi. Activities include litter collection, tree planting, and spreading awareness about recycling.
